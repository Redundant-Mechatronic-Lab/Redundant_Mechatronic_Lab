Advancing Core Technologies for Manufacturing

Engineering with precision | Open by principle



At Redundant Mechatronic Lab, we are dedicated to the design and development of advanced manufacturing tools, emphasizing precision, performance, and scalability. Our work is driven by a deep technical understanding of mechatronics, materials, and control systems, enabling the creation of highly optimized industrial solutions.

We believe that transparency and accessibility are key to meaningful innovation. While we respect intellectual property and industry-specific licensing, we actively contribute to the open-source ecosystem, sharing knowledge and methodologies that benefit both independent developers and industrial partners.

Many of our projects are available under two compartments: open-access and licensed collaboration models, allowing for flexible adoption and integration into commercial and research applications. We welcome partnerships, contributions, and discussions on technology development and optimization. If you're interested in working with us, get in touch.
